Professional Baseball

Where is Professional Baseball Played?

The main places where professional baseball is played are as follows:

  • MLB and MILB in the US
  • KBO in Korea
  • NPB in Japan
  • Winter Leagues in the Dominican Republic (LIDOM) and Puerto Rico (LBPRC)
  • The LMB in Mexico
